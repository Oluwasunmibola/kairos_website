About Kairos Christian Fellowship


Welcome to Kairos Christian Fellowship, a community of believers devoted to experiencing the fullness of God's time and purpose in our lives. "Kairos," a Greek word meaning "appointed time" or "opportune moment," reflects our commitment to recognizing and embracing the divine moments orchestrated by God.



Our Mission:


At Kairos, our mission is to passionately follow Christ, cultivate genuine relationships, and impact the world with the love and message of Jesus. We believe in creating an atmosphere where people can encounter the transformative power of God's grace.


Our Vision:


Our vision is to be a thriving fellowship where individuals are equipped to live purposeful lives, rooted in faith, and empowered to make a positive difference in their communities. We aspire to build a family of believers who walk together in spiritual growth, love, and unity.


Core Beliefs:


  • We believe in the authority of the Bible as God's inspired word.
  • We believe in the redemptive work of Jesus Christ and salvation through faith in Him.
  • We embrace the Holy Spirit's guidance for personal and communal transformation.
  • We are committed to worship, prayer, and the pursuit of a vibrant relationship with God.

Join Us:


Whether you are seeking a place of worship, community, or spiritual growth, Kairos Christian Fellowship welcomes you with open arms. Come as you are, and let's journey together in God's Kairos moments.


Connect With Us:

Visit our gatherings, engage in fellowship, and explore the exciting opportunities to grow in your faith. Follow us on social media to stay updated on events, teachings, and ways to get involved. We look forward to meeting you and experiencing God's Kairos moments together! Blessings, The Kairos Christian Fellowship Family
